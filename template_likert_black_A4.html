About Data Literacy

The Data literacy assesment (DL) is an open research project created at Business University in Prague. In cooperation with industry professionals, DL has been developed to offer simple yet powerful ways to measure something never before measurable: An individual's data literacy level, by relating it to five traits linked to individual data literacy capability.

About Competencies

Datal literacy methodology compose of five assessed levels:

  • Section A "Data concepts, ethics and protection" evaluate respondents ability to understand the role, characterists of data and basic concepts of data management. This component also measure the awarness of data ethics principles and legal limitations.
  • Section B "Analytical principles and methods" evaluate knowledge of basic approach to problem-solving with data and ability to recognize visualization techniques. Knowledge of analytical methods used in business is also measured by this section.
  • Section C "Data collection and preparation" evaluate respondents ability to identify relevant data sources and ability to access, extract and store data. This section also measure ability to recognize data quality or transforming data to target structure.
  • Section D "Data analysis and evaluation" evaluate respondents ability to apply analytical and visualization methosd morover to interpret graps. This section also measure ability of dimensional modeling.
  • Section E "Analytical principles and methods" evaluate knowledge of basic approach to problem-solving with data and ability to recognize visualization techniques. Knowledge of analytical methods used in business is also measured by this section.
